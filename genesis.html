DPSJ 10 Year Transformation

DPS Genesis

From Church High School in 1941 to DPS Jamnagar today — a journey of growth, excellence and values.

Early Beginnings

The journey began in the President’s Estate, later evolving into Naveen Bharat School, and finally Delhi Public School Jamnagar.

Growth & Milestones

  • 1941: Classes began at President’s Estate.
  • 1949: Relocation and expansion of facilities.
  • 1960s-1990s: Labs, library, and athletics developed.
  • 2000s onwards: Digital classrooms, life skills programs, co-curricular excellence.

Philosophy

DPS Jamnagar educates the whole child academically, morally, and socially, emphasizing curiosity, collaboration, and responsibility.
